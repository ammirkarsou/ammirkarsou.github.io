<strong>Authors:</strong> <strong>Ammir Ayman Karsou</strong>

<strong>Advisor:</strong> Wagner Moreira Lupinacci

<strong>Program:</strong> BSc in Geophysics (capstone thesis)

Explores <strong>viscoacoustic modeling</strong> and <strong>AVO anomalies</strong>, contrasting the <strong>full Zoeppritz equation</strong> with linearized approximations. The thesis analyzes attenuation (Q) impacts, elastic property contrasts, and approximation limits on lithology and fluid diagnosis.

<strong>Highlights</strong>
- Quantitative comparisons between Zoeppritz and common approximations (e.g., Shuey).
- Sensitivity assessment of AVO responses to elastic contrasts and attenuation effects.
- Synthetic scenarios illustrating how *gathers* behave under different contrasts.
