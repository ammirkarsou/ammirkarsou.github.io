Combines a receiver-extension FWI framework with Bayesian weighting to mitigate non‑repeatability issues in time‑lapse (4D) acquisition. Validated on Marmousi and a Brazilian pre‑salt case, reducing 4D noise and improving time‑lapse estimates.
